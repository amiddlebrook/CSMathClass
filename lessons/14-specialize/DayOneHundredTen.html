Section 1

Ricci Flow

The "heat equation for geometry". Deforms a manifold over time to smooth out curvature.

\frac{\partial g_{ij}}{\partial t} = -2 R_{ij}

Used by Perelman to prove the Poincaré Conjecture.

Section 2

Minimal Surfaces

Surfaces that locally minimize area (like soap films). They have zero mean curvature (`H=0`).

Section 3

Navier-Stokes Existence

Clay Millenium Problem: Do smooth solutions to 3D Navier-Stokes exist for all time?

Or do they develop singularities (blow up)?

Section 4

Python: Heat Equation

Python 1D Heat Diffusion 
import numpy as np
import matplotlib.pyplot as plt

# Explicit Finite Difference
u = np.zeros(100)
u[40:60] = 1.0 # Initial heat pulse
k = 0.4 # diffusion coefficient

for t in range(100):
    u[1:-1] += k * (u[2:] - 2*u[1:-1] + u[:-2])
