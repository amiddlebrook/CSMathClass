Section 1

Choosing a Field

Don't just choose what's "hot". Choose what you find yourself thinking about in the shower.

Follow your curiosity, not the hype cycle.
Section 2

Reading Papers

Research papers are not textbooks. They are dense.

  • Pass 1: Title, Abstract, Introduction, Conclusion. Get the main idea.
  • Pass 2: Main definitions and theorem statements. Treat proofs as black boxes.
  • Pass 3: Deep dive. Reproduce steps.
Section 3

The T-Shaped Learner

You now have a broad foundation (the horizontal bar of the T). Now you must go deep in ONE area (the vertical bar).

It is better to be an expert in one thing than mediocre in everything.

Section 4

Join the Community

Mathematics is social. Attend seminars (even if you don't understand). Talk to people. Post on StackExchange. Write a blog.
