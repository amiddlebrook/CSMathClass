Key Concepts

Days 28-37 covered: Logic, Sets, Relations, Induction, Counting, Recurrences, Graphs, Trees, Coloring.
LOGIC • Propositional: ∧, ∨, ¬, →, ↔ • Quantifiers: ∀, ∃ • De Morgan: ¬(A∧B) = ¬A∨¬B RELATIONS • Reflexive, symmetric, transitive • Equivalence relation = all three • Partial order = refl + antisym + trans INDUCTION • Base case + inductive step • Strong induction: assume all preceding • Structural induction for recursive structures COUNTING • Sum rule, product rule • P(n,k) = n!/(n-k)!, C(n,k) = n!/k!(n-k)! • Binomial theorem: (x+y)ⁿ = Σ C(n,k)xᵏyⁿ⁻ᵏ RECURRENCES • Characteristic equation for linear recurrence • Master theorem: T(n) = aT(n/b) + f(n) GRAPHS • V - E + F = 2 (Euler's formula) • Tree: connected + acyclic + |E| = |V| - 1 • Bipartite: no odd cycles • BFS/DFS: O(V + E)
