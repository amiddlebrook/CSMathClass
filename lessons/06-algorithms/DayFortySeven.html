Las Vegas vs Monte Carlo

LAS VEGAS: Always correct, random running time (e.g., Randomized QuickSort) MONTE CARLO: Fixed time, may be wrong (e.g., Miller-Rabin Primality)
Las Vegas gambles on time, Monte Carlo gambles on correctness.
