Section 1

Graphing & Functions Review

The visual language of code analysis.

Scales

Linear: Constant growth ($y=x$).

Log-Log: Power Laws (Polynomails) become lines. Used for Big O.

Semi-Log: Exponentials become lines. Used for Moore's Law.

Asymptotes

Vertical: The "Crash". Division by zero. $x \to c$.

Horizontal: The "Limit" ($x \to \infty$). Steady state capacity.

Section 2

Trig Review (Cycles)

The circle of life (and signals).

The Big 3 Concepts:
1. Unit Circle: Cosine is X, Sine is Y. That's it.
2. Identity: $\sin^2 + \cos^2 = 1$. (Distance is 1).
3. Dot Product: A measure of "Parallelism" or Similarity using Cosine.
Python Tip: Audio processing (FFT) converts Time Domain (Sample array) to Frequency Domain (Magnitude array).
