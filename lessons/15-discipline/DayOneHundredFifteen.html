Section 1

Consistency > Intensity

It is better to study for 30 minutes every day than for 10 hours once a month.

Mathematics is a language. Use it daily or lose it.

1.01^{365} \approx 37.8, \quad 0.99^{365} \approx 0.03
Section 2

Active vs Passive Learning

The Illusion of Competence: Watching a video or reading a solution makes you feel like you understand. You don't.

You only understand if you can solve the problem blank sheet of paper, without looking at hints.

Section 3

Getting Stuck is Good

Being stuck is not a sign of stupidity. It is the sensation of your brain building new neural pathways.

Embrace the struggle. If you aren't stuck, you aren't learning.
