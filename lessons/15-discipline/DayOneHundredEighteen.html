Section 1

Breadth-First Search

Many students get stuck on Line 1 of Page 1. They try to do a Depth-First Search (DFS) of the textbook.

Instead, do a BFS. Scan the chapter. Understand the landscape before you explore the caves.

Section 2

The First Pass

Goal: Memorize the Definitions and Theorem Statements.

Do NOT read the proofs. Do NOT do the hard problems. Just learn the language.

If you don't know what "Compact" means, you can't prove anything about it.

Section 3

Building the Map

By the end of Pass 1, you should have a mental map of the subject. You know what is true, even if you don't know why.
