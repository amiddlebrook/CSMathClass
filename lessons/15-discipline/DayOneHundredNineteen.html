Section 2

Reconstructing Proofs

Do not read proofs. Reconstruct them.

Read the theorem statement. Close the book. Try to prove it. If you get stuck, peek at the first line of the proof, then try again.

Section 3

Doing Hard Problems

The only way to verify you understand is to solve problems you haven't seen before.

Apply Polya's method: Understand, Plan, Execute, Reflect.
